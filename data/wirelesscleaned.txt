WILLIAM PATERSON UNIVERSITY WIRELESS CONNECTION GUIDE
================================================================================

GENERAL WIRELESS CONNECTION
---------------------------
**Windows/Mac OS/iOS**:  
1. Select "WP Wireless" from available networks.  
2. Enter credentials when prompted.  
3. Accept certificate warnings.  
4. Connect.  

**Chromebook/Android**:
**Network Name:** WP Wireless or WP Wireless (5Ghz)  
**Security:** 802.1x EAP  
**Settings for All Devices**:
- EAP Method: PEAP  
- Phase 2 Authentication: MSCHAPV2  
- Domain Suffix: ise.wpunj.edu  
- Identity: Your WP Connect username  
- Password: Your WP Connect password  
- Anonymous Identity: Leave blank  


**Troubleshooting**:  
- Android: Disable "Randomized MAC" (use "Device MAC" or "Static MAC").  
- Chromebooks: Forget all networks (Eduroam, WP-Wireless Guest) first.  
- Contact Help Desk: https://help.wpunj.edu/helpdesk | 973-720-4357  


SPONSORED GUEST ACCESS
----------------------
**For faculty/staff to sponsor guest access**:  
1. Visit: http://www.wpunj.edu/sponsoredguest  
2. Log in with WP credentials.  
3. Click "Create Account" and fill in guest details (default: 24-hour access).  
4. Share credentials with guest.  

**Guest Instructions**:  
1. Connect to "WP Wireless-Guest".  
2. Open browser, visit WPUNJ.edu.  
3. Log in with provided credentials.  

**Note**:  
- Do NOT use "WP Wireless-Guest" on personal phones/laptops.  
- Gaming consoles/streaming devices MUST use "WP Wireless-Guest".  


DEVICE REGISTRATION (GAMING CONSOLES, SMART DEVICES)
----------------------------------------------------
1. Register at: http://mydevices.wpunj.edu  
2. Click "Add".  
3. Enter:  
   - Device Name (e.g., Xbox, Roku).  
   - MAC Address (wired/wireless).  
   - Description (optional).  
4. Restart device and connect to "WP Wireless-Guest".  

**Note**:  
- MAC addresses are required for registration.  
- Registration may show "Pending"; connect anyway.  


FINDING MAC ADDRESSES
---------------------
**Amazon Echo**:  
- Use Alexa app > Device Setup > Wi-Fi step (MAC appears before connecting).  

**Amazon Fire/Apple TV/Roku/Chromecast**:  
- Settings > About/Network > Locate MAC.  

**Gaming Consoles**:  
- **PlayStation**: Settings > System Information.  
- **Xbox**: Network Settings > Advanced Settings.  
- **Nintendo**: Internet/System Settings > MAC Address.  

**Guides**:  
- Video Tutorials: Linked in original document.  


UNSUPPORTED DEVICES
-------------------
- **Wireless Printers**: Not supported (use USB).  
- **Chromecast**: Limited functionality (enable Guest Mode).
